# Content Rating Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Target Profile; Official Rating Pending

ZUNO Battle targets players aged 13 and older. During development the product
may display only this neutral label:

> TARGET AUDIENCE 13+ — OFFICIAL STORE RATING PENDING

It must not display the ESRB `T`, PEGI `12`, IARC, Google Play, or another
official rating badge until the applicable authority or storefront assigns it.

## Target content boundaries

- stylized fantasy animal combat
- non-graphic melee and energy-weapon impacts
- no blood, gore, dismemberment, exposed organs, or lingering injury detail
- defeated enemies dissolve, retreat, or become harmless rather than suffer
- no hate content, sexual content, gambling, drugs, or strong profanity
- purchases and online features disclosed accurately in the store questionnaire

The release manager must complete the relevant storefront/IARC questionnaire
using the final build's actual content and interactive elements. Any added
realistic violence, user communication, purchases, location sharing, or
user-generated content triggers a new rating and safety review.
